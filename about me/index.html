ABOUT ME

A sucker for...

    Freshly baked bread, clean linen sheets, and the warm wet smell of the rain in the heat.
    Powdered sugar, faintly sweet. Flaky buttery croissants. Thick dark cups of coffee.

    Diving into a perfectly temperatured indoor pool, the stinging heat of a warm house after a few hours in the snow (inviting but still biting).

    TV on low in the background while sleeping next to somebody. The buzz of a periwinkle blue refrigerator light at 3 am.

    The silver light that floods through sheer curtains just before the sun rises when it seems like the entire world is quiet and revolving around you. Blurry city lights through a wet windshield.

    Love that looks like skinâ€¦ so close you can see every pore, every freckle, every pimple, every scar. And damn does it look beautiful.



I find inspireation in...

    People. Details. Dirty fingernails ripping up napkin corners. 5 year old shoes tapping on a tile floor. Drug use. Drug recovery. First look. Last kiss. Street lights. People who meet as strangers at a party and then sleep together. New cities. Chinese take out. Tattoo stories. Accents. Scars. Shame. Regret. Innocence. The way people fix their own hair. The fuzzy blue-ish lights in dark bathrooms. Teeth. Crooked teeth. Straight teeth. Gritted teeth. Smiles. People.



Fun for me means...

    Doing something unusual, finding a furious love story, a sudden moment of epiphany, a way to cure my itch for the transcendental.

    Trying to solve the mysteries of the blood, a way to return to the innocence, the way out of the labyrinth.
